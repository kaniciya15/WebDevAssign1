WELCOME TO OUR ZOO

History of our ZOO


National(Dehiwela) Zoological Gardens of sriLanka was found in 1920s by John Hagenbeck. Although it officially started operating in 1939. Our zoo is one of the oldest zoo in Asia.
In 1973,the zoo had:

  1. 158 Mammal species
  2. 259 Bird species
  3. 56 Reptiles
  4. 7 Fish species

Our ZOO Now


Over 1 million local and tourists visit the zoo every year.And also many school and university student visit here for educational purpose. We loved to say we have redevelopment programmes to enhance the facilities of the zoo for both animals and the visitors.

  1. 72 Mammal species
  2. 65 Bird species
  3. 31 Reptiles
  4. 89 Fish species
  5. 3 families of Alphibians
  6. 30 Butterfly species
  7. There are around 453 species of avifauna in our Zoo.

Visiting our ZOO


Our zoo will provide you and your family an endless entertainment. You can share unique stories of each animal and birds with your beloved ones while walking around our zoo.The zoo also teaches you about the importants of conservation of all the animals and birds.
